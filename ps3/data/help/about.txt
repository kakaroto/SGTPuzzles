                        About SGT Puzzles v0.1

This Puzzle Games Collection is a port of Simon Tatham's Portable
Puzzle Collection for the Playstation 3 System and it includes
33 addictive puzzle games for your enjoyment.

The port of these games to the Playstation 3, as well as the
Playstation 3 front end was written by Youness Alaoui (KaKaRoTo).

These games are distributed under the MIT licence.

Please visit http://humblehomebrew.com to sign a Petition asking Sony
for official Homebrew support, and donate to the authors of these
games as well as the Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF).

Thank you, and enjoy these games!
